About Me

As you can see my name is Joshua Silvernale. For the longest time I didn't know what I wanted out of life. I was just going along without any real direction. I went to college for a couple of years, and after those years I realized that school, in its traditional sense, was just not for me. I also worked at ralphs from age 15 to age 21. While I was going to school and working at Ralphs I had a lot of fun. I'd do things with friends a lot and it would always be a blast, but I was lost. For six years I was lost. It was in that sixth year that I decided I couldn't keep doing this! I had no money and no career! My decision was to become a personal trainer! My career started at crunch fitness. I worked there for a while before I started training independantly. This is where I met the love of my life and the woman that I would eventually get married to. After working as an independant trainer for a few years I started to realize that training isn't what I wanted to do with the rest of my life. So I went back to crunch so I could have a steady place to work while I figured out what I was going to do. Before I knew it though, the pandemic was here. Oddly enough this is when my girlfriend, at the time, and I decided to get married and move our lives to Texas. Even though I didn't want to train anymore it was the only thing I was qualified to do. So in order to go to Texas we both needed to find jobs and a place to live. This is when I started working at Cannon Fitness. I was there for about a year and a half when I started to get that feeling again that I needed out of this industry. It just wasn't making me happy. I quit and started working at a company called Starpak. While there I was looking into this "coding" thing. I had no idea what it was or what it entailed. As I was looking into it I found that it's on the computer! I loved being on the computer. Also, that a specific field uses it to develop video games! Let me tell you. I played video games A LOT back in the day and I mean PLAYED VIDEO GAMES. I lost my life to World of Warcraft for about five years, I played a game called Maple Story for a couple years and I lived on x-box live for a few years. So there it was. Software engineering it is! I enrolled myself at coding temple and have been learning ever since. What I've learned so far I have absolutely fallen in love with! I couldn't be happier, and I know I'll be a great asset to any company that I work for!
